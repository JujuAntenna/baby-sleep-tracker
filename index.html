ℹ️ How It Works

Prediction: The sweet spot is when your baby is most likely ready for their next nap, based on their actual patterns from the past week combined with age-appropriate wake windows.

Earliest/Latest: The range is based on how consistent your baby's patterns have been. Wider range = less predictable.

Schedule Pressure: 🟢 means the day is on track to hit bedtime. 🟡 means things are getting tight. 🔴 means the schedule is off — consider adjusting.

Nap Decision: When it's unclear whether to squeeze in another nap or go to early bedtime, the app shows both options with trade-offs.

Morning Plan: At the start of each day, you'll see proposed 2-nap and 3-nap schedules. The app adapts the plan as naps happen.

Day Rating: Rate each day to help the app learn which patterns work best for your baby.

Data: Everything is stored locally in your browser. Export regularly as a backup!
